How to Play
1
The chase. Dycson runs toward the Light on the right. The Darkness chases him from the left — if it catches him, it's game over.
2
Answer to advance. A Bible question appears in this panel with a countdown bar. Tap an answer before time runs out.
3
2 tries per question. A wrong pick is marked and you can try again once more. After your 2nd miss or a timeout, the correct answer and its scripture reference are revealed.
4
Correct = light. Right answers push the Darkness back, calm it down, and brighten your world. Wrong answers (or timeouts) let it surge closer and grow angrier.
5
Stronghold of Doubt. Every 5th round is a timeline puzzle — tap events in the bank in the correct chronological order, then hit "Break Wall". You also get 2 attempts here.
6
Streak shields. A 3-question streak earns Armor of Light, which absorbs the penalty of your next miss. A 10-streak earns it again.
7
Eras & scoring. Every 5 questions advances you to the next biblical era, from the Patriarchs to Revelation. Your Mind Renewal Score is saved to the Scoreboard when you finish.
